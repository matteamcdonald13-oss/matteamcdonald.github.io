Why I Love Flowers

Flowers have captivated me for as long as I can remember. I love that flowers come in all shapes and sizes, and each have their own quirks about them. From the delicate curves of a tulip to the lush fullness of a peony, every bloom tells its own story. There's something magical about working with flowers. The way colors complement each other, how textures create depth, and the satisfaction of creating something that makes people smile. It's a perfect blend of creativity, nature, and thoughtfulness.

My Favorite Flowers

Tulips

My absolute favorite! Tulips are elegant, simple, and come in the most stunning array of colors. Their clean lines and graceful shape make them perfect for any arrangement, from casual bouquets to formal centerpieces.

Peonies

Peonies are absolutely luxurious with their full, ruffled blooms. They have a romantic quality that's hard to match, and their fragrance is simply divine. Working with peonies always feels special.

Hyacinths

Hyacinths bring such a delightful fragrance and unique texture to arrangements. Their clustered blooms and vibrant colors add wonderful depth, and they signal the arrival of spring like no other flower.
